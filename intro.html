Welcome to Roadworks IRE

Where Performance Meets Passion

Roadworks IRE is more than just a automotive enthusiast group - it's a community bound by a shared passion for performance, precision, and the pure joy of driving. Based in Ireland, we're dedicated to showcasing the finest builds, sharing knowledge, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible on four wheels.

Our Mission

We believe that every car has a story, and every build is a journey. Our mission is to document these journeys, celebrate the craft, and inspire the next generation of automotive enthusiasts. From track-focused builds to street machines, we cover it all with the same level of passion and attention to detail.

What We Do

  • • Featured Builds: We showcase exceptional automotive builds from across Ireland, highlighting the creativity and technical expertise of our community.
  • • Track Days: Regular track events where enthusiasts can safely explore the limits of their machines in a controlled environment.
  • • Technical Content: In-depth articles, guides, and tutorials covering everything from basic maintenance to advanced performance modifications.
  • • Community Events: Meet-ups, shows, and gatherings that bring together like-minded enthusiasts to share their passion.

Join the Journey

Whether you're a seasoned builder, a weekend warrior, or simply someone who appreciates automotive excellence, there's a place for you in the Roadworks IRE community. Follow our journey through our blog, connect with us on social media, and be part of something special.
