Josh's S15: Three Years in the Making

January 26, 2024 • 3 min read • Noah - Roadworks IRE

Where to begin…

This shoot is about 3 years in the making. Growing up there wasn't many Irish car YouTube channels and Juicebox was one. The first video I ever watched was Josh's E30 being driven for the first time. Fast forward a couple years I met him at the first LZ World Tour where he had unveiled the S15 for the first time. We hit it off from there and now with me finished school and having my license it gave me the ability to call down to himself and Flip to shoot the 15.

The Build

The car itself is one of the nicest S15 in Europe and arguably the world. Featuring a M-Sports front bumper and Vertex side skirts and rear bumper complemented by canards, it gives it the aggressive appearance that I and so many others love. It sits perfectly on a set of colour matched SSR Type-C's. The huge GT Style wing just adds to the early 2000's Japanese style.

The Interior

The cabin features a Juran GTX100 and Recaro and keeping you in place is a V Factory 4 point harness. The dash dodger adds to street drift car looking whilst also providing a massive safety feature.

Under the Hood

Under the bonnet the SR20 has been appropriately tuned to 360hp with its rare mid-mount turbo set up giving the S15 its unique sound.

The Details

The addition of the G-Corporation stickers, the D1 window banner, Defi Gauges, eye lids, M-Sports Plate, Garage Midnight stickers etc just perfect it.

Josh (Juicebox) has really built himself an absolute savage S15, I count myself very fortunate to have been able to shoot it and even get a little go at the summer bash. Thank you Josh for allowing me to shoot it and as always thanks to everyone and anyone who has read this far.

// Noah
