Sean's Honda Civic EP3 Type R

February 20, 2026 • 2 min read • Noah - Roadworks IRE

I was given the opportunity to drive my good mates EP3 Type R a couple of weeks ago. This thing is the definition of an anti christ.

Sean has spared no expense with this build. Under the bonnet is a 260hp K24 Frank built by no other than @ismail_kira over in @nightshift_honda. The engine features Tegiwa air intake and a Tegiwa manifold into a full custom exhaust by @conormccann96 and mapped by @cianwalshmotorworks.

The interior remains fairly mild (for now), but the rear seats have been deleted to save weight. The car sits on a lovely set of Evo 8 Enkeis whilst Yellowspeed Coliovers off an Ex BTCC Touring car keep the car planted round Mondello.

Maxton Designs Kit adds enough of a lip round the car to give it a more edgy look. I can't thank Sean enough for entrusting me with his EP3. A man not scared to drive it on.

As always, thank you all for reading this far. Big things planned for year, plans are in motion // Noah
