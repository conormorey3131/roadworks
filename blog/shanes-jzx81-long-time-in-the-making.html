A shoot that was a long time in the making, Shane's JZX81

January 27, 2026 • 2 min read • Noah - Roadworks IRE

A car the originated in Japan, exported to Texas, imported to Galway and then into Shane's hands. Shane's fitted the kit in recent times and having had the 1J built to 530hp. It sits nicely on its RPF1's and on the rear.

One of my favourite cars this island has to offer. Once again I'd like to thank Shane for shooting the car and having me down and to anyone who has read this far too. Until then, Noah
