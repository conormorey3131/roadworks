Summer Nights

February 24, 2026 • 2 min read • Noah - Roadworks IRE

It's night like these I miss most about the summer. Plans made at the last moment, seeing who's about and what they want to do. The most fun happens on nights without a plan. A bit of food and a spin up the hills to try catch the sun before it disappears. I'm looking forward to this summer. Hopefully the weather plays ball and I'll be able to do more photoshoots, organise spins/meets and enjoy the show season.

This was only one of the many nights that I brought my camera with me to photograph the cars that live close to home. I'm lucky to be surrounded by a fantastic bunch of lads that aren't afraid to use their toys, it's what they're built for. Garry's IS200 is a previous feature car on the page which has done 2 trips to the ring and back and the same with Ciaran's EP3 which I hope to feature soon. Graeme's Levin is just a perfect example of a Twincam. A fully restored example from top to bottom which I hope to feature someday soon too. As is always, thank you to those who have read this far // Noah
